The backstory of “metamorphosis” “Metamorphosis” started as a scientific communication with Harvard University that never reached the general audience. The communication had a groundbreaking impact, so groundbreaking that the communication was hurted with unbearable obstacles. We decided to disrupt the normal flow of scientific communications riddled with policies and take the communication to the general audience. We reworked the communication into a style adapted to the general audience. That restyling ended-up in an artistic universe where deep science is versed into poetry and geometries. Don’t be misled, “The Nikouo Art” is profoundly a scientific communication. And the reader will have an emotional journey, drowned into the “Nikouo” universe.
